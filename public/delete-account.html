Request Account Deletion

We are sorry to see you go. To delete your OWODE Alajo account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

How to delete your account:

  1. Send an email to support@owodealajo.com
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered phone number in the email
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email when your account is deleted

What data will be deleted?

  • ✅ Your profile information (name, phone, email)
  • ✅ Your BVN/NIN verification data
  • ✅ Your facial verification data
  • ✅ Your wallet and transaction history
  • ✅ Your Ajo group memberships

What data will be retained?

  • ⚠️ Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations (CBN guidelines)
  • ⚠️ Any pending legal or regulatory obligations
⚠️ Important: If you have a pending Ajo payout, please collect it before requesting account deletion. Deleting your account will forfeit any pending payouts.
